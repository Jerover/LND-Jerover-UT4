Masters at our things!

We have been creating our hamburgers since 1970, we select our products of the best possible quality, fresh native vegetables and fruits, matured meats and free-range chicken.

We serve all palates!

Don't you like any hamburger on our menu? We have a section in our menu where you can choose what type of bread you want, what type of meat, the point, its quality, as well as what ingredients and sauces you want to add to your delicacy, up to a total of 6.

Vegans are welcome!

We have different types of vegan meats, Heura provides us with endless products for you, also delicious cheeses and sauces. Do not worry about cross contamination, we have plates and utensils reserved only for your products.

To infinity and beyond!

We are a company in expansion, we already have up to 4 locations throughout Las Palmas de G.C, but we would like to continue growing, if you want to set up your own Grasita Burger franchise, contact us through our social media or our email.
